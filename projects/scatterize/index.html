Scatterize

2011

Demo site • Example dataset • Project page

Scatterize is a project designed to change the way researchers interact with statistical data. In it, researchers upload a spreadsheet containing their data, and choose the relationship they want to see. A plot of the data in the relationship and statistical significance parameters are instantly visible. Researchers can then click on data points to exclude them from the analysis, interactively seeing how that affects significance. One researcher remarked that Scatterize saved more than ten hours per analysis task.

In this project, I was responsible for all aspects of design and implementation.
